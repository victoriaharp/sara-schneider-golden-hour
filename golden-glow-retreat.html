Golden Glow Retreat

The 3-Day Golden Glow Retreat will take you from getting by to glowing November 7-10, 2024. In this busy world full of demands, perhaps you are feeling unfocused, unregulated, and unfulfilled. Or maybe you are looking to live your life with more intentional joy, even amid the really hard stuff. The Golden Glow Retreat is your chance to reset and refocus. Sara will speak to living in the now and holding your current circumstances while building, planning, and striving for more. More joy, more fulfillment, and more peace. The retreat includes beautiful accommodations, every delicious meal, a fabulous massage experience, 4 group coaching sessions, one private coaching session, and surprises along the way.
