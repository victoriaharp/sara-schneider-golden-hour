A Life Coach...

is a professional who helps individuals identify and achieve their personal and professional goals. Life coaching is a thought provoking, creative, and collaborative process where a certified coach works with a client to assess their current situation, explore their desires and aspirations, and develop strategies and action plans to achieve their goals.

Sara coaches clients...

seeking a greater sense of joy in various areas of life, including career, relationships, health, personal growth, and overall well-being. She helps clients develop self-awareness, enhance their strengths, improve their mindset, and take practical steps towards achieving their goals, with joy!
Life coaches do not provide therapy or counseling, but rather focus on helping clients set and achieve goals in the present and future. Sara believes that individuals have the potential to create positive change in their lives, and that she, as a skilled coach, can facilitate that process by providing guidance, support, and motivation. Sara’s coaching sessions can be conducted through one-on-one sessions via phone or Zoom sessions. The duration of coaching engagement can vary depending on the goals and needs of the client.
